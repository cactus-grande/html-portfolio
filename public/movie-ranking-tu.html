The Best Movies according to Tu

My top 3 movies of all time

Howl's Moving Castle

A movie of Studio Ghibli with the setting based on a village in Colmar, Alsace. The main characters are well-developed, the story plot is well-thought and the animation is top-notch.

Before Series

A series featuring a couple in a meet-cute scenario on a train to Austria, and they decided to walk through the city of Vienna together while getting to know each other. Bittersweet with a touch of realism.

Everything, everywhere, all at once

A movie from A24 production, with a touch of surrealism. The movie revolves around the livelihood of Asian immigrants, and takes the audience through the relationship lense between a mother and daughter in her teenage years.
